Sanitized substrate from a versioned-beta personal handoff (Phase 2 of [pitch-deck-field-guide project](https://github.com/bamboo-DCM/bamboo-marketing/blob/main/active/pitch-deck-field-guide/pitch-deck-field-guide.md)). Cross-links applied: `SKILL.md` "Using the kit beyond this skill" + "When NOT to fire" reference `field_guide.md` for greenfield-deck redirects; `playbook.md` opening section points pre-deck founders to `field_guide.md` first; `cowork_workflow.md` carries a Step-0 callout near the top of the guide.
